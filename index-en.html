Masseria Prontera: three Apartments with Pool and extensive Garden

The villa with its traditional tuff vaulted ceilings is over 100 years old and was completely restored in 2017.

The living area has about 220 square meters and offers up to 14 beds. The house is divided into three independent apartments.

All apartments have their own fully equipped kitchen, bathroom, living room and roofed terrace.

The villa is located in the center of a small southern Italian village. The absolute central location (5 km from the Adriatic Sea and 5 km from the Ionian Sea) is ideal for reaching all major attractions in a few minutes by car.

On the east side are the most beautiful sandy beaches of Salent (Le Maldive del Salento). Eight kilometers away on the west side, the cliffs of Ponte Ciolo or the impressive caves of Le Tre Porte.
